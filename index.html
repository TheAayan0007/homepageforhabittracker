Customization

Make it completely yours

Every habit, icon, schedule and color can be tailored. Here's what you can customize:

🎯
Managing Habits
Add a habit: Click ＋ Habit in the top bar on the Habits tab. Enter a name and an emoji icon.
Edit a habit: Go to My Habits → click ✏ Edit next to any habit. Change name or icon.
Delete a habit: Click ✕ on any habit card. Completion history is preserved in your JSON.
Reorder habits: Drag the ⠿ handle up or down. Order reflects in all views instantly.
📋
Managing Tasks
Add a task: In Weekly view, click the + button on any day card, type your task, press Enter.
Complete a task: Click the task row to toggle its checkbox. Completed tasks get strikethrough.
Delete a task: Hover the task row — a red ✕ appears on the right. Click to remove.
View progress: The donut chart on each day card shows task completion % live.
⏱
Using Timers
Global timer: Click ⏱ Timer in the topbar. Choose a preset or enter custom H:M:S. A ring countdown appears.
Habit timer: On the Habits page, click the ⏱ icon on any habit for a per-habit popup.
Clock alarm: Set a specific time (e.g., 07:00 AM) — the app fires a sound notification when reached.
Countdown: Choose presets 5–60 min or custom duration. Ring animates down to zero then sounds.
📊
Reading Charts
Daily Progress: Bar/line chart of daily completion % across the current month.
Weekly Progress: Average completion per week, great for spotting slumps.
Per Habit: Side-by-side bars showing which habits you're nailing vs. neglecting.
Overall Stats: Pie chart showing total completed vs. remaining across the month.

completionsobjectMap of "YYYY-MM-DD" → array of habit IDs completed that day
