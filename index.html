How it works
One file.
Any device.
Always synced.
Your entire tracker — habits, completions, tasks, streaks — lives in a single file called habittrack-data.json stored directly in your Google Drive. Every change syncs automatically within seconds. No manual saving needed.

Google Drive Sync — Complete Guide
Everything you need to know about saving and syncing your data
Saving to Drive
1
Auto-save on every change
When signed in, every action (ticking a habit, adding a task, navigating months) auto-saves to Drive after 1.5 seconds. No button needed.
2
Manual save via 💾 Save → Google Drive
Click the Save button in the topbar → choose "Save to Google Drive" to force an immediate save and see a confirmation toast.
3
Save Locally as backup
Click 💾 Save → "Save Locally" to download the JSON file to your device as a manual backup anytime.
File name: habittrack-data.json — stored in the root of your Google Drive. You can see it, move it, back it up, or share it like any other file.
Loading from Drive
1
Auto-load on sign in
When you sign in with Google, the app automatically finds your habittrack-data.json in Drive and loads all your data instantly.
2
Import via 📂 Open → Google Drive
Click the Open button → "Import from Google Drive" to manually pull the latest version from your Drive at any time.
3
Switch accounts
Click the account avatar (top-right) → click any listed account to switch. Each account has its own separate Drive file.
New device? Just open the app and sign in with your Google account. Your entire history loads automatically — habits, completions, tasks, everything.

Customization

Make it completely yours

Every habit, icon, schedule and color can be tailored. Here's what you can do:

🎯
Managing Habits
Add a habit: Click ＋ Habit in the top bar on the Habits tab. Enter a name and an emoji icon.
Edit a habit: Go to My Habits → click ✏ Edit next to any habit. Change name or icon.
Delete a habit: Click ✕ on any habit card. History is preserved in your JSON.
Reorder habits: Drag the ⠿ handle up or down. Order reflects in all views instantly.
📋
Managing Tasks
Add a task: In Weekly view, click the + on any day card, type your task, press Enter.
Complete a task: Click the task row to toggle its checkbox. Completed tasks get strikethrough.
Delete a task: Hover the task row — a red ✕ appears. Click to remove.
View progress: The donut chart on each day card shows task completion % live.
☁️
Google Drive Sync
Sign in: Click Continue with Google on welcome screen or the account avatar (top-right).
Auto-save: Every change auto-saves to Drive within ~1.5 seconds. Watch the status bar turn ☁ Synced.
Switch accounts: Click the account avatar → click any account in the list. Each has its own Drive file.
New device: Just open the app and sign in — all data loads from Drive automatically.
⏱
Using Timers
Global timer: Click ⏱ Timer in the topbar. Choose a preset or enter custom H:M:S.
Habit timer: On the Habits page, click the ⏱ icon on any habit for a per-habit popup.
Clock alarm: Set a specific time (e.g., 07:00 AM) — fires a sound notification when reached.
Countdown: Choose presets 5–60 min or custom duration. Ring animates down to zero then sounds.
JSON KeyTypeWhat it controls
habits[].namestringThe display name of the habit shown in all views
habits[].iconstringEmoji icon (e.g. "🏃") shown next to the habit name
completionsobjectMap of "YYYY-MM-DD" → array of habit IDs completed that day
tasksobjectMap of "YYYY-MM-DD" → array of {text, done} task objects
vm / vynumberThe last-viewed month (1–12) and year — restores your view on next open
